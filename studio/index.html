Ferra Content Studio

Ferra Content Studio is the internal content tool of the Ferra brand. We use it to plan, schedule and publish our own short-form videos (Reels and YouTube Shorts) to our own social media accounts, and to read the performance of our own posts so we can improve our content over time.

How it uses Google services

With the account owner's permission, the app connects to our own Google and YouTube account and uses:

  • YouTube Data API – upload auth/youtube.upload
    To upload our own videos to our own YouTube channel.
  • YouTube – read access auth/youtube.readonly
    To read our own channel and video statistics.
  • Google Drive auth/drive
    To detect the new video files we place in our own Drive folder so they can be published.

The application only accesses our own accounts and data. It does not access other users' data, and it does not sell or share any data with third parties. For questions or data-deletion requests, contact admin@ferra.es.
